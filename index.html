Our Story

More Than Just a Bar

BS Bar & Grill is a locally owned sports bar located on St. Charles Rock Road in St. Ann, Missouri. Known for fast homemade lunch specials, affordable wing deals, and nonstop entertainment — we proudly serve the greater St. Louis area including Bridgeton, Overland, and those working near Lambert Airport.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch, meeting friends after work, joining a dart league, or catching the big game — BS Bar & Grill delivers a welcoming neighborhood atmosphere with cold drinks and great food.

Beyond our weekly events, you'll find us hosting theme nights, bike nights, DJs, benefit nights, fundraisers, and more. Check our events calendar or just walk in — there's always something going.
